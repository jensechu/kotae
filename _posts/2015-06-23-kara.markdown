## Meaning

from _____.

---

## Usage

If after a clause can indicate:

* as a **result** of this
* to come **from** a location

---

## Examples

大丈夫です**から**心配しないでください。
I am fine **so** don't worry about me.


内**から**会社にいきました。
I went **from** home to my office.
