## Meaning

to try to _____.

---

## Usage

ーて form verb.

{% ruby ["見", "み"] %}る means 'to see'. In this case 'to see' the modified verb carried out.

---

## Examples

{% ruby ["私", "わたし"] %}はビールを{% ruby ["飲", "の"] %}んで**みる**。
I only drink beer.
