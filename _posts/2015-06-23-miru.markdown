## Meaning

to try to _verb_.

---

## Usage

ーて form verb.

{% ruby ["見", "み"] %}る means 'to see'. In this case 'to see' the modified verb carried out.

---

## Examples

{% ruby ["私", "わたし"] %}は{% ruby [["日", "に"], ["本", "ほん"], ["語", "ご"]] %}を{% ruby ["話", "はな"] %}して**みる**。
I try to speak Japanese.
