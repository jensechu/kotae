## Meaning

Asking for permission

may I _verb_.

Giving permission

you may _verb_.

---

## Usage

ーて form verb.

---

## Examples

{% ruby ["犬", "いぬ"] %}を{% ruby ["撫", "な"] %}でて**もいい**です。
It is okay to pet the dog.

{% ruby [["写", "しゃ"], ["真", "しん"]] %}を{% ruby ["撮", "と"] %}って**もいい**ですか。
May I take a pictre?
